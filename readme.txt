Improve your Google PageSpeed scores: Optimize your website's resources and have a fully optimized WordPress site in 3 clicks using the PageSpeed.Today plugin.

== Description ==

WordPress allows for quite a lot of flexibility when it comes to achieving great loading speed and Google PageSpeed scores. PageSpeed.today takes care of the main optimization issues affecting your score and makes optimizing your website qucik and easy.

> <strong>Premium Features and Support</strong><br>
> The PageSpeed.today team provides active support to both free and premium users. One-on-one email support is available for people who bought the [Premium PageSpeed.Today plugin](https://pagespeed.today) only.
> Note that the Premium PageSpeed.today plugin removes the daily limits on page scans and optimization. Freemium users will be limited to 25 scans and 5 page optimizations per 24 hours as this process creates high server load requiring vast CPU resources.

= Improve your Google PageSpeed Score with PageSpeed.today =
Using our global scan, get the latest PageSpeed scores for all of your website's pages and optimize images, minify CSS and minify Javascript resources in a click of a button.

= Page Scan =
The PageSpeed.Today WordPress (from here on referred to as "PSTWP") plugin allows you to easily get the latest PageSpeed scores for all of the pages of your website. It's important to keep track on them to make sure they all adhere to Google's principles of a professional website.

= Page Optimization =
The PSTWP allows you to optimize your images, minify your CSS files and minify your JavaScript resources in a click of a button. Bulk Optimization option is provided to Premium users.

== Installation ==

=== From within WordPress ===
1. Visit 'Plugins > Add New'
2. Search for 'PageSpeed.Today'
3. Activate PageSpeed.Today from your Plugins page.
4. Go to "after activation" below.

=== Manually ===
1. Upload the `pagespeed-today` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the PageSpeed.Today plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to "after activation" below.

=== After activation ===
1. Visit 'Settings > pagespeed.today.
2. Go to plugin's 'Settings' and activate the options that you need optimized.
3. Visit 'Optimization' tab to scan and optimize your website's pages.
